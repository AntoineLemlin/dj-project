About DJ Angerone

Mike Myers, professionnaly known as DJ Angerone, is internationally acclaimed Progressive House & Trance DJ & musician.

I try to experiment with various music genres in every track that I make.

Since the beginning of his career in 2010, he has already released 5 EPs on range of worldwide known labels (Booster Records, DSTRCT Recordings etc.) and received a number of music awards.
